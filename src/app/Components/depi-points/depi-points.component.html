FAQ:

How do I earn DEPI Points?

DEPI Points are earned by participating in our various activities such as completing coding challenges, submitting projects, and engaging with the community. Each action has its own point value, which gets added to your total points.

How do I redeem my points?

Redeeming your points is easy! Once you've collected enough points, simply visit the rewards section below and select a reward that suits your needs. Your points will be deducted upon redeeming.

When will my points be reflected in my account?

Redeeming your points is easy! Once you've collected enough points, Points will be reflected within 48 hours of completing a task or activity. Make sure your submissions and activity logs are accurate to ensure timely updates.
